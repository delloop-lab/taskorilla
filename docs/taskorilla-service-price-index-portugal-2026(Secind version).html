Taskorilla Service Price Index

Ref: PT-2026-V2 | Issued: 2nd Quarter 2026

This document establishes the 2026 Taskorilla Service Price Index (TSPI) for the Portuguese market. It serves as a non-binding benchmark for users to calibrate service expectations against verified market liquidity data and national labor indices.

Figures are synthesized from aggregated platform transactions, regional economic fluctuations, and compliance with the 2026 Portuguese self-employment (Recibos Verdes) tax framework. Typical prices are presented as gross totals; individual VAT (IVA) application depends on each provider's specific tax threshold under Article 53.

This Index focuses on general assistance and domestic tasks. It excludes services provided by Professional Helpers or certified tradespeople. Regulated activities (e.g., licensed electrical installations, structural engineering, or certified medical care) are subject to independent professional rate cards, mandatory liability insurance, and distinct VAT regulations under Article 151 of the PIT Code (CIRS).

2026 Market Commentary

"The 2026 TSPI reflects a national average increase of EUR 1.50 - EUR 3.00 per hour across manual service sectors compared to 2025. This shift is primarily driven by the EUR 920 Minimum Wage adjustment and increased operational costs (fuel/transport) in urban hubs like Lisbon and Braga."

National Minimum Wage vs. TSPI Baseline (Index Marker)

This TSPI baseline is an index reference only, not a recommended helper take-home wage. Final rates are independently negotiated and are typically higher based on scope, travel, urgency, and skill level.
